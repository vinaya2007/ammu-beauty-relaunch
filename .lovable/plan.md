

# Ammu Beauty Care — Premium Beauty Parlour Website

A stunning, multi-page website for a 4.8-star rated ladies parlour relaunching in Moolapalayam, Erode. Premium feminine design with deep maroon, gold accents, cream tones, and elegant typography.

---

## Design System
- **Colors**: Deep maroon primary, gold accents, cream/soft beige backgrounds, white cards
- **Typography**: Elegant serif headings (Playfair Display), clean sans-serif body text
- **Style**: Rounded cards, soft shadows, fade-in scroll animations, subtle gradient overlays
- **Layout**: Mobile-first, fully responsive, sticky navbar with smooth scroll

---
